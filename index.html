How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one! You win if you make a tile with the value 2048. You lose if the board gets full and you have no more valid moves.
Board Size: You can set the dimensions of the board by entering a number from 3-10 in the Board Size text box and clicking Submit.
Extra Life: Merge two extra life tiles to gain an extra life. In case you lose, a life will automatically be spent to clear some of the board and you will be able to continue the game.
Explode: Merge two explode tiles to get a bomb item. Click on the bomb item, then click anywehere on the board to explode a 3x3 area of tiles.
Trim: Merge two trim tiles to get a trim item. Click on the trim item to remove all tiles with values of 2 or 4 from the board.
Next Tile: Each turn, you are shown a preview of the next tile that will be added to the board after you make your next move.
Held Tile: Click Hold to save the Next Tile for later. After waiting at least 3 turns, you can click Hold, then click anywhere on the board to play the held tile onto the board.
